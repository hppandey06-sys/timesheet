⚡ Power Automate — Daily 11am Email Reminder Setup
Free with Microsoft 365
📧 This flow checks every morning at 11am who has not logged hours for the previous working day and sends them an automatic email. It runs daily until they fill in the timesheet.
Step 1 — Create a SharePoint List called "TimesheetSubmissions" Go to your SharePoint site → New → List. Add columns: EmployeeEmail (text), SubmitDate (date), Hours (number). Each time an entry is saved, it appears here.
Step 2 — Go to make.powerautomate.com Sign in with your Microsoft 365 account → Click Create → Scheduled cloud flow
Step 3 — Set the trigger Flow name: Timesheet Daily Reminder · Start: Tomorrow · Repeat every: 1 Day at 11:00 AM · Click Create
Step 4 — Add "Get items" action from SharePoint Site: your SharePoint site · List: TimesheetSubmissions · Filter Query: SubmitDate eq '[yesterday's date]'
Step 5 — Add "Apply to each" → check if email is in results Loop through your team member list. For each member, use a Condition: "Does their email appear in yesterday's submissions?" → If NO → send reminder email.
Step 6 — Add "Send an email (V2)" for the NO branch To: [member email] · Subject: ⏱ Reminder: Please fill your timesheet
Copy-paste email body:
Hi [Employee Name], This is an automated reminder that your timesheet for [Yesterday's Date] has not been submitted yet. Please log your hours here: [YOUR TEAMS TAB URL] You will continue to receive this reminder each morning at 11am until your timesheet is filled. If you have a leave or holiday, please inform your manager so they can mark it accordingly. Thank you, HR / Management Team
